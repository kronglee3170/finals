Founded in 1946, in Brooklyn, New York

Cosmic Fitness was founded in 1946 in Brooklyn, New York, as a space where people from different backgrounds and stories come together with one goal - staying healthy.

Established just after World War II, Cosmic Fitness started as a small local gym aimed at helping returning veterans regain their strength and well-being. Over the decades, it has become a stable of the community, welcoming individuals from all walks of life.

From humble beginnings with simple weights and training equipment, Cosmic Fitness has grown into a modern fitness facility that blends history and innovation. We pride ourselves on creating a space where inclusivity, health, and community meet. Today, Cosmic Fitness stands as a testament to the belief that fitness is for everyone, regardless of age, gender, or experience. Our mission has been the same since day one: to empower people to achieve their fitness goals in a supportive and welcoming environment.
